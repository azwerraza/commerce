Our Delivery Policy

At Timeless Trend, we ensure your order reaches you as quickly and safely as possible. Please read our delivery guidelines below.

  • Standard Delivery Time: 2–5 business days within Lahore, and 5–10 days nationwide.
  • Shipping Charges:
    • Free delivery on orders above PKR 2,000
    • PKR 200 for orders below PKR 2,000
  • Delivery Areas: We deliver all across Pakistan through trusted courier services.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available nationwide.
  • Order Tracking: A tracking link will be shared via SMS/email once your order is shipped.
  • Public Holidays: Deliveries may be delayed during national holidays or extreme weather conditions.

For any questions regarding your delivery, please feel free to contact us.
